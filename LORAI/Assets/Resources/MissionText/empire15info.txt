<b>Information:</b>
Refer to the mission rules for instructions for the <b>X-8 blaster droids</b>. 

<b>Special Setup:</b>
Deploy three Custom Groups as the <b>X-8 blaster droids</b> and assign them colors. The app activates and exhausts them.

<b>Special Rules:</b>
When a group of <b>X-8 blaster droids</b> is defeated, do not mark them as defeated in the app. If they activate before they are redeployed, ignore their activation and proceed to the next Imperial activation.

<b>Repositioning Target:</b>
Block access to the terminals.

<b>Rebel Victory:</b>
All terminals are destroyed and there are no more <b>X-8 blaster droids</b> in the factory.

<b>Imperial Victory:</b>
At the end of Round 6, or all heroes are wounded.
